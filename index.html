Our philosophy

Care that moves at the pace of the body, not the clock.

Amara means grace — and it's the standard every treatment is held to. We blend Yoruba herbal traditions with modern spa technique, using shea, black soap, and botanicals sourced from markets across the South-West.

No two bodies hold tension the same way, so no two sessions are identical. Your therapist reads what you need that day and shapes the ritual around it — not the other way round.

— The Amara Spa team

How a visit unfolds

Every session follows the same unhurried arc, from the door to the last cup of tea.

01

Arrival & consultation

A short conversation about tension points, allergies, and pressure preference, over chilled hibiscus water.

02

Warm foot soak

Ten minutes in a rosemary-infused soak to ground you before the treatment room.

03

The treatment

Your chosen ritual, delivered at your pace, in a private room with dimmed light and low sound.

04

Tea & reflection

A closing cup of ginger-lemongrass tea in the courtyard before you re-enter the day.
